Production-grade observability and evals for the teams quietly shipping the LLM features the rest of the world is still pitching.

What we actually do

Helix lives between your LLM provider and your product. Every prompt, response, tool call, and embedding is captured, scored against custom evals, and monitored for regressions, hallucinations, drift, and cost anomalies — the moment they happen, not the moment a customer tweets about them.

Eval-as-code in Python and TypeScript runs on every production trace, not just CI. The bar is simple: catch the broken AI flow before the customer does.

Who we sell to · ICP

  • Buyer: VP Engineering, Head of AI, or Platform Eng Lead.
  • Champion: a senior ML / Platform / AI infra engineer who has been burned by a silent regression.
  • Geo: North America and Western Europe. Fully remote sales motion.
